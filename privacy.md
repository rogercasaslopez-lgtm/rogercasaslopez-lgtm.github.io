Privacy Policy — Pablo Cocktails Barcelona
Effective date: 29 de marzo de 2026

1. General information
Pablo Cocktails Barcelona is a cocktail guide application for Android. This privacy policy describes how we handle information from users of the application.
We do not collect, store or transmit directly identifiable personal data such as name, email address or phone number. No account creation is required to use the application.
2. Locally stored data
The application stores locally on your device: cocktails marked as favourites, ratings you assign to cocktails, and the status of the premium purchase. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.
3. Advertising (AdMob)
The free version of the application displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. Google may use device advertising identifiers to display personalised ads. You can consult Google's privacy policy at: https://policies.google.com/privacy
Users who purchase the Premium version will not see any advertisements and AdMob will not be initialised for them.
4. In-app purchases (Google Play Billing)
The premium purchase is managed entirely through Google Play. We do not store payment data. We only store locally on your device an indicator that the purchase has been made. You can consult Google Play terms at: https://play.google.com/about/play-terms/
5. Permissions
The application does not request permissions to access the camera, microphone, location, contacts or any other sensitive device data.
6. Minors
The application is intended for users over 18 years of age, as its content is related to alcoholic beverages. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.
7. Changes to this policy
We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new version on this page with a new effective date.
8. Contact
If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can contact us through the application's page on Google Play.
